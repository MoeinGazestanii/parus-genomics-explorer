Genomic Resources: Parus Genus

This website provides public access to genomic resources of birds from the Parus genus. Parus is a genus of old-world birds in the tit family. Its most known member is the great tit (Parus major), which is also the species represented by the most samples on this website.

We provide a database of sequence data and the corresponding metadata to facilitate collaboration between researchers and provide access to the public.
